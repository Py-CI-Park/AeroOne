# v1.7 Requirements

1. Source tier chart text must not overlap or be clipped.
2. Source tier chart must not require horizontal scrolling.
3. Local official PDF archive and source registry must be shown as one unified registry.
4. Aircraft verification-grade rules must move to the top of Sources.
5. Color rules must be presented as a systematic legend.
6. Portal segment/verification charts must not have title-box overlap.
7. Previous user-facing distribution files may be deleted after v1.7 validation to save capacity.
8. All v1.6 aircraft data, PDFs, Wide Catalog and Radar behavior must remain unchanged.
